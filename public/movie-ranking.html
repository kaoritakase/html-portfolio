Kaori's Top Anime Movies

My top 5 anime movies.

Spirited Away

A classic. I have watched it so many times since I was little! I love how you can feel Japanese culture through the movie, and also Chihiro's journey to become stronger, resulting in her rescuing her parents.

Howl's Moving Castle

As with many other Ghibli movies, I love how they can portray ugly and beautiful together. A "normal" girl helping a "strong" magician! Loved it.

Your Name

The artwork and animation are beautiful! And the story is cute and intriguing.

Princess Mononoke

As a Ghibli fan, I love strong female characters and this movie portrays that very well. Plus the Japanese historical influence that it has is portrayed amazingly.

My Neighbor Totoro

Watched it countless times, the story is cute but dark. This movie made me a Ghibli fan.
